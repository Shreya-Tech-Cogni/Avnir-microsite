What's in it for businesses?

Till now Real Estate was largely dependent on buyers to visit the property and physically experience the space which along with a lot of other potential red-flags have veered buyers away in the light of COVID 19. All of 2020 has highlighted the blind spots of the real estate industry and we have envisioned an interactive and immersive digital solution for the whole industry that will enable immersive personalised experience for buyers. Expand your sales by displaying all your properties and inventories to all your prospective buyers anywhere across the globe 24x7.

Sale the deal

Housing sales are estimated to have plunged down drastically over the last few months , forcing developers to offer discounts and compromise on payment plans, hence affecting businesses.

We have envisioned a platform to build digital experience, to enable virtual property exploration, to provide an interactive and immersive experience and help users to find their future homes.
